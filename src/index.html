About me

Originally from Cooperstown, New York, I began my journey into Software Engineering on a different route than most. For nearly a decade I followed in the the footsteps of my great- grandfathers and built a career as a professional blacksmith. During that time, I also recieved my Bachelor's Degree in American Studies and Native American Studies.

A few more years passed and I decided to look for opportunities to be a creator in other facets and looked to App Academy to help me get started in the field of Software Engineering. An intense coding bootcamp, App Academy taught me multiple coding languages and frameworks required to be a proficient full-stack engineer over the course of 16 weeks. In addition to technical skills, App Academy also helped be build a solid foundation in git workflow, data management systems, and over 1,000 hours of pair-programming and team-based development.

Boblin's Adventure Guide

Boblin's Adventurer's Guide is a comprehensive data visualization platform presenting rules and statistics for the 5th edition of Dungeons & Dragons by Wizards of the Coast. Constructed utilizing Vanilla JavaScript, webpack, and npm for efficient project dependency management, the application seamlessly integrates with an external API. Leveraging DOM Manipulation techniques, it dynamically renders fetched data to provide users with an immersive experience.

Betsy

Betsy is a replication of the renowned ecommerce platform Etsy. Users on Betsy can create profiles, review products, and explore the site's inventory. It is built using React and Redux for frontend functionalities, and Ruby on Rails for backend development. Data management is handled by PostgreSQL as the primary database, with media rendering managed by Amazon Web Services (S3).
