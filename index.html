About me

Originally from Cooperstown, New York, I began my journey into Software Engineering on a different route than most. For nearly a decade I followed in the footsteps of my great-grandfathers and built a career as a professional blacksmith. During that time, I also received my Bachelor's Degree in American Studies and Native American Studies.

A few more years passed and I decided to look for opportunities to be a creator in other facets and looked to App Academy to help me get started in the field of Software Engineering. An intense coding boot camp with a 5% acceptance rate, App Academy taught me multiple coding languages and frameworks required to be a proficient full-stack engineer over 16 weeks. In addition to technical skills, App Academy also helped me build a solid foundation in git workflow, data management systems, and over 1,000 hours of pair-programming and team-based development.

I currently hold a contracting position as a Software Developer in a backend role at Regulatory Solutions Inc. where I consulted and helped initialize a MongoDB database alongside the lead engineer into the existing architecture in order to offer a stateless database solution for storing relevant customer data. Alongside my primary role at Regulatory Solutions Inc., I am also engaged as a contract Software Engineer at DataAnnotation, where I evaluate AI chatbot models for their proficiency in responding to coding prompts written in Python and Javascript.
